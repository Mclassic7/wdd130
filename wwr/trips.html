Trips Stages

Stage One

Stage one is built for first-timers or beginners. This 3-hour trip includes basic training and a safety orientation.

Difficulty: Easy
Duration: 4 hours
Price: $79/person

Stage Two

Stage two is designed for intermediate rafters who are ready for a more challenging experience. This trip includes advanced training and a guided rafting adventure.

Difficulty: Medium
Duration: 6 hours
Price: $129/person

Stage Three

Stage three is for experienced rafters seeking the ultimate adventure. This full-day trip includes expert-level rapids and breathtaking scenery.

Difficulty: Hard
Duration: 8 hours
Price: $199/person
