Rivers State

"Rivers State is situated in the southern part of Nigeria, in the Niger Delta region. It is bordered by Imo and Abia States to the north, Akwa Ibom State to the east, Bayelsa and Delta States to the west, and the Atlantic Ocean to the south. The capital of Rivers State is Port Harcourt, which is an important industrial and commercial center, particularly known for its oil and gas industries."
